About Me

I'm Hassan Eid — a data scientist with a background spanning over 10 years in operations, sales, and healthcare recruiting. After leading teams and scaling businesses in industries ranging from insurance to staffing, I transitioned into data science to combine my analytical mindset with modern tools like Python, machine learning, and SQL.

My experience at 4 Geeks Academy helped me deepen my technical foundation while mentoring others. I've worked on projects in predictive analytics, regression modeling, and NLP — and I enjoy solving problems where business meets data.

I bring a unique edge: the ability to communicate clearly with stakeholders, identify the real business need behind the data, and deliver actionable insights.

I'm based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and speak English (native) and Arabic (intermediate). When I’m not coding, I’m usually helping others grow, spending time with family, or learning something new.
